Chili Recipe

Description

An easy and delicious chili recipe for a busy weeknight. This recipe only takes about ten minutes to prepare and 20 minutes to cook. Pair with cornbread for a declious dinner. Serves six.

Ingredients

    1lb ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 15oz tomato sauce
  • 15oz kidney beans
  • 14.5oz stewed tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1tsp chili powder
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
