Pumpkin Pie Recipe

This easy pumpkin pie recipe is perfect for this fall season!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup cold butter or shortening
  • 3tbsp cold water

Filling

  • 2 cups mashed, cooked pie pumpkin
  • 12oz evaporated milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp groudn nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
